SHORT DESCRIPTION (under 300 chars)
------------------------------------
A cyberpunk choose-your-destiny RPG with 3,000+ story nodes, 10 playable races,
D&D-style combat, and hundreds of branching paths. Every choice reshapes your fate
in a megacity where death is cheap and trust is expensive.

------------------------------------
LONG DESCRIPTION (About This Game)
------------------------------------
LATENCY is a massive text-based RPG set in a dystopian megacity where humanity
coexists uneasily with orcs, elves, cyborgs, and beings from beyond the void.

CHOOSE YOUR IDENTITY
Pick from 10 unique races — Human, Orc, Wood Elf, Dark Elf, Dwarf, Half-Giant,
Cyborg, Synth, Shadowkin, or Voidborn. Each comes with distinct racial abilities,
stat bonuses, and origin stories that shape how the world treats you.

FORGE YOUR PATH
Six career paths await: Enforcer, Hacker, Smuggler, Medic, Gladiator, or Diplomat.
Your job determines your contacts, your reputation, and the doors that open — or
slam shut — as you navigate the megacity's power struggles.

CHOOSE YOUR ALLEGIANCE
Five factions vie for control: the Iron Collective, the Neon Court, the Circuit Saints,
the Ghost Syndicate, and the Ashen Circle. Earn their trust or their hatred. Play them
against each other. Or burn them all down.

REAL CONSEQUENCES
With 3,000+ story nodes and hundreds of branching paths, your choices matter.
Stat checks use D&D-style d20 mechanics — your character build determines what
you can attempt, and the dice determine if you succeed. Failed rolls don't just
block progress; they open entirely different storylines.

FIGHT DIRTY
Turn-based combat with attack rolls, damage dice, abilities, items, and the option
to flee when the odds turn against you. Every enemy has unique AI behavior — some
are aggressive, some tactical, some cowardly.

FEATURES
- 3,000+ story nodes across a 5-act campaign
- 10 playable races with unique origin stories
- 6 career paths with dedicated storylines
- 5 factions with reputation systems
- D&D-style d20 combat with dice rolling
- Character stats, skills, inventory, and equipment
- Multiple endings based on your choices
- Atmospheric cyberpunk soundtrack
- Voice narration (browser text-to-speech)
- Autosave + 5 manual save slots
- Achievement system
