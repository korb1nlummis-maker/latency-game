ACT 2-3 SCENES (4 images)
===========================
Mid-game story progression.

39. martial-law-rooftop
    Used in: Act 2, Chapter 1 opening
    A Lower District rooftop view three months after the riot. Meridian drones in
    tight formation overhead. Scarred blast marks on buildings below. The rebuilt
    Transit Hub now a military fortress. Rust-colored dawn light. The city under
    occupation. Oppressive, surveilled, wounded.

40. convoy-ambush-night
    Used in: Act 2, Chapter 2 opening
    An armored freight convoy ablaze at night in a narrow industrial corridor. Iron
    Collective soldiers pouring from warehouse doors. EMP grenades sparking blue.
    Cutting torches on hauler doors. Smoke, fire, organized violence. A heist in
    progress.

41. vault-descent
    Used in: Act 3, Chapter 1 opening
    A figure at the bottom of a deep vertical shaft. An ancient facility stretching
    into darkness below. Cold ancient air. Seamless alloy walls with faint blue
    circuit patterns beginning to glow as something wakes up. Pre-collapse
    technology. Alien, ancient, dangerous.

42. vault-atrium-pods
    Used in: Act 3, Chapter 1
    A vast circular atrium rising through twelve levels. A central column of
    crystalline storage arrays pulsing blue. Twelve coffin-sized pods at its base,
    three containing preserved bodies with cables running from their skulls into
    the column. The secret the city was built on.


FACTION HEADQUARTERS (5 images)
=================================
Each faction's home base.

43. iron-collective-sublevel
    Used in: Iron Collective faction storyline
    Sub-level nine of the Foundry. Blast furnaces tall as buildings. Rivers of
    liquid metal flowing in floor channels. Hundreds of heat-suited workers in
    orange industrial haze. Raw industrial power. Hell on earth, but it's THEIR
    hell.

44. cloud-towers-platform
    Used in: Neon Court faction storyline
    Breaking through the cloud ceiling into actual sunlight. Crystalline Cloud
    Towers rising like pillars of glass. Prismatic rainbow reflections. Glass
    skywalks between towers. A holographic fountain of pure upward-flowing light.
    Heaven above the dystopia. Breathtaking, exclusive, untouchable.

45. circuit-saints-relay
    Used in: Circuit Saints faction storyline
    An abandoned relay station at night. A half circuit-board, half-crucifix
    holographic cross glowing on the roof. A grey-robed woman with glowing
    cybernetic spiral-iris eyes standing at the entrance. Technology as religion.
    Reverent, eerie, hopeful.

46. ghost-syndicate-surveillance
    Used in: Ghost Syndicate faction storyline
    A basement room lit by sixteen monitors showing security feeds of every
    district. A figure in a featureless reflective black mask sitting at the
    center. White-noise washing machines stacked along one wall (for audio
    masking). Total information control. Paranoid, powerful, invisible.

47. ashen-circle-ceremony
    Used in: Ashen Circle faction storyline
    A circular underground chamber lit by hundreds of real candles. Twelve robed
    ash-grey figures in a circle. A kneeling woman holding a gleaming cortical
    stack freshly extracted from her own skull. A small hammer waiting beside her.
    Willing destruction of identity. Cult horror, devotion, terrifying beauty.


ACT 4-5 CLIMAX (5 images)
============================
Endgame scenes.

48. city-on-fire-morning
    Used in: Act 4, Chapter 1 opening
    The megacity at dawn with three columns of black smoke rising from different
    districts. An entire block burning orange. An overturned security transport
    below with "LATENCY KILLS" spray-painted on it. Military drones overhead.
    The war has begun. Apocalyptic, beautiful, terrifying.

49. summit-archive-dawn
    Used in: Act 5, Chapter 1
    A brutalist concrete cathedral (the Municipal Archive) at rust-colored dawn.
    Smoke hanging in the still air. Exhausted faction soldiers from all five
    factions converging on the entrance. Different faction banners visible. The
    last hope for peace. Weary, desperate, historic.

50. five-leaders-table
    Used in: Act 5, Chapter 1
    Five faction leaders around a circular scarred wood-and-iron table in a
    vaulted reading room. Each distinct: armored Commander Vex (Iron), elegant
    Archon Luminara (Neon), cybernetic Ezra-7 (Circuit), masked Broker (Ghost),
    painted Hierophant (Ashen). Power, tension, five different visions for the
    future.

51. ghost-nexus-data-cathedral
    Used in: Act 5, Chapter 2
    A cathedral-sized space made of holographic data streams cascading from
    ceiling to floor like luminous waterfalls. A masked figure on a levitating
    platform at the center. Information architecture visible as glowing
    structures. The Ghost Syndicate's true power revealed. Awe-inspiring.

52. blaze-of-glory-last-stand
    Used in: Combat endings
    A lone figure making a last stand against overwhelming enemies in a confined
    space. Weapon raised. Bodies already on the ground around them. Defiant
    stance. Dramatic backlighting through smoke. Going down fighting. Heroic,
    tragic, unforgettable.
