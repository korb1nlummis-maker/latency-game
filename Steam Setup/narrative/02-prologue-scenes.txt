PROLOGUE SCENES (7 images)
============================
These appear during the shared prologue that all races play through.

24. slum-apartment-wake
    Used in: Prologue node_001 "The Lower District"
    A tiny rust-stained room with a cot, metal shelf, cracked mirror. Rain streaking
    down a grimy window. The megacity stacked vertically outside. A Meridian
    holographic ad glowing across the street. The worst apartment you've ever seen,
    but it's yours. Cramped, lonely, rain-soaked.

25. lower-district-street-rain
    Used in: Prologue node_006 "The Street"
    A narrow neon-lit street canyon between massive tower blocks in acid rain. Vendor
    stalls under tarps. Patrol drones overhead with searchlights. A street preacher
    on a crate. Crowds in hoods pushing through. Cyberpunk street life at its grittiest.

26. vendor-stalls-black-market
    Used in: Prologue node_007 "The Vendor Stalls"
    A row of Lower District vendor stalls under corrugated awnings. Black-market
    augmentations laid out on velvet. Data chips in anti-static bags. Ration packs.
    A kid reaching for someone's pocket. Hustle, desperation, survival commerce.

27. mako-repair-shop
    Used in: Prologue node_009 "Mako's Repair Shop"
    A cluttered cyberpunk repair shop interior. Hanging cables, gutted electronics
    everywhere. A compact woman with augmented mechanical arms working at a
    workbench. Neon sign buzzing "MAKO'S" in the window. Organized chaos, warmth
    amid the grit. She's your first ally.

28. alley-mugging-standoff
    Used in: Prologue node_016 "The Alley"
    A dark rain-soaked alley between buildings. Three augmented street thugs blocking
    the path — one with a metal jaw and crackling shock baton, one with a blade, one
    cracking knuckles. Electronic waste in dumpsters. Menacing neon backlighting.
    This is your first fight.

29. job-board-crowd
    Used in: Prologue node_020 "The Job Board"
    A covered intersection with an 8-foot industrial display screen bolted to a
    concrete pillar. Scrolling colored job listings in terminal font. A crowd of
    desperate job seekers pressing forward, elbowing for position. Your ticket out
    of nothing.

30. stack-dealer-encounter
    Used in: Prologue node_026 "The Stack Dealer"
    A clean, well-dressed man in a dark coat at the edge of a rain-slicked square.
    Holding an open matte-black case with a glowing memory stack inside — blue-white
    light illuminating his face from below. Neon reflections on wet ground. Corporate
    menace disguised as opportunity.
