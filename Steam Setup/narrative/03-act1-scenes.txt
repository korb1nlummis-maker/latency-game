ACT 1 SCENES (8 images)
=========================
Job introductions and the Act 1 climax.

31. foundry-gates-fortress
    Used in: Act 1, Enforcer chapter opening
    The Iron Collective's Foundry — a fortress of corrugated iron and reinforced
    concrete bristling with jury-rigged turrets and searchlights. Two armored guards
    at a massive gate. Industrial smoke rising. Military-industrial compound in the
    slums. Intimidating, powerful, brutal.

32. foundry-training-yard
    Used in: Act 1, Enforcer chapter
    A vast industrial cathedral interior repurposed as a training yard. Dozens of
    fighters drilling with bladed weapons. Sparks from welding. Propaganda murals
    on the walls. A massive banner with a barbed-wire fist logo. Boot camp meets
    factory floor.

33. deep-net-cafe
    Used in: Act 1, Hacker chapter opening
    A basement bunker lit in ultraviolet. Rows of signal-dampened privacy booths.
    Patrons jacked into terminals with slack bodies and twitching fingers. Ozone
    and solder smell. Cold blue-purple atmosphere. The dark web made physical.

34. neon-court-entrance
    Used in: Act 1, Smuggler chapter opening
    A matte-black featureless door in an unmarked building. A single violet neon
    line pulsing along its frame like a vein. Mid-district alley setting. Understated
    elegance hiding extreme wealth. You'd walk past it and never know.

35. neon-court-lounge
    Used in: Act 1, Smuggler chapter
    A sleek underground lounge with low furniture, dark polished surfaces. Violet
    and blue ambient lighting. A dozen people negotiating in whispers. A figure in
    a neon-thread suit approaching with a drink. Luxury, danger, style.

36. medical-quarter-clinic
    Used in: Act 1, Medic chapter opening
    A converted storage-unit clinic. Cots along both walls with patients. A harried
    triage station. Hand-painted sign reading "ALL WELCOME - NO STACK REQUIRED."
    Organized chaos, compassion in a brutal world. Warm despite the poverty.

37. registration-crisis-broadcast
    Used in: Act 1, Chapter 4 climax
    Every holographic billboard and screen in the city simultaneously showing
    Director Voss's face in a charcoal suit. Crowds below staring up in horror.
    The moment mandatory registration for all non-humans is announced. Dystopian
    propaganda broadcast. Orwellian dread.

38. transit-hub-riot
    Used in: Act 1, Chapter 4 climax
    A transit hub engulfed in chaos. Overturned barriers. Drone searchlights cutting
    through tear gas. Crowds surging against security forces in riot gear. Orange
    fire, broken glass, water cannons. The city breaking apart. Visceral, violent,
    desperate.
