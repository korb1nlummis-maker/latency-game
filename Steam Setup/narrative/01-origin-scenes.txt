ORIGIN CUTSCENE SCENES (23 images)
====================================
These appear during the 10 race origin cutscenes at the start of the game.

1. human-duct-scrubbing
   Used in: Human origin, slide 4
   A young human laborer on hands and knees scrubbing a narrow, grimy ventilation
   duct lit by a single headlamp. Hands cracked and bleeding, chemical residue on
   metal walls. Claustrophobic, oppressive. Dark tones with harsh white headlamp light.

2. human-bunk-room-grief
   Used in: Human origin, slide 7
   A cramped communal bunk room with thin walls. A hand pressed flat against the wall.
   Dim emergency lighting. Forty bunks stacked tight in industrial housing. Lonely,
   suffocating atmosphere. Blue-grey tones.

3. human-cityscape-dawn
   Used in: Human origin, slide 9
   A figure silhouetted at a housing block threshold at synthetic dawn. The megacity
   stretches vertically above in tiers of steel, neon, and cold light. The moment of
   stepping out into the world. Warm orange dawn vs cold blue city.

4. orc-foundry-smelting
   Used in: Orc origin, slide 2
   Interior of a massive industrial foundry. Molten orange metal pouring from a
   crucible. An orc mother shielding a child strapped to her back. Intense heat haze,
   sparks, sweat. Orange and black palette with industrial scale.

5. orc-foreman-knockout
   Used in: Orc origin, slide 10
   A muscular orc standing over a crumpled human foreman on a foundry floor. Shock
   prod on the ground. Every orc worker on the line has turned to watch in silence.
   Dramatic moment of defiance. Low angle, dramatic lighting.

6. orc-gate-departure
   Used in: Orc origin, slide 11
   An orc walking through industrial factory gates into a rain-soaked megacity street.
   Blood drying on knuckles. Foundry sirens wailing behind, red warning lights. Walking
   away from everything they knew. Rain, neon, determination.

7. wood-elf-surveyor-flags
   Used in: Wood Elf origin, slide 4
   Corporate surveyors in orange vests planting a Meridian Industries flag at the edge
   of a green grove. Spray-painted trees marked for cutting. Scanner equipment. Tension
   between nature and corporate machinery.

8. wood-elf-grove-clearing
   Used in: Wood Elf origin, slide 6
   Industrial cutters chewing through a forest growing from cracked concrete ruins.
   Birds fleeing. Sawdust and diesel in the air. Wood elves with bows watching from
   branches above. Nature being destroyed by machines.

9. wood-elf-mother-tree-falls
   Used in: Wood Elf origin, slide 8
   A massive mutant oak crashing down at sunset. Roots ripping through sublevels.
   Green dust filling the air like snow. Elves keening in silhouette against orange
   sky. Devastating, emotional. The death of something sacred.

10. dark-elf-creche-darkness
    Used in: Dark Elf origin, slide 2
    A lightless underground hall where dark elf children learn to navigate by touch.
    Faint bioluminescent markings on tunnel walls. Cool limestone atmosphere. Near-total
    darkness with subtle blue-green bioluminescence.

11. dark-elf-mentor-blade
    Used in: Dark Elf origin, slide 3
    A dark elf mentor (female, named Yvenne) materializing from tunnel shadow behind a
    young student. Blade tip pressing against the student's spine. Teaching by ambush.
    Sharp features, silver-white hair, predatory grace.

12. dark-elf-surface-emergence
    Used in: Dark Elf origin, slide 11
    A hooded dark elf emerging from a tunnel mouth into the sodium-lit sprawl of the
    lower districts at night. Blade at hip. Crowd of surface dwellers ahead. Eyes
    adjusting to harsh artificial light after a life underground.

13. dwarf-grandmother-workshop
    Used in: Dwarf origin, slide 1
    Close-up of an elderly dwarf woman's broad, scarred, oil-blackened hands holding a
    wrench with surgical precision over a water reclamation unit. Workshop tools
    surrounding her. Reverent, skilled, generations of knowledge in those hands.

14. dwarf-pipe-burst-repair
    Used in: Dwarf origin, slide 9
    A dwarf chest-deep in floodwater in a dark service tunnel. Plasma torch clenched
    between teeth. Both hands bracing a massive burst pipe. Sparks flying into dark
    water. Heroic, desperate, skilled under pressure.

15. half-giant-barracks-bunk
    Used in: Half-Giant origin, slide 2
    A row of reinforced steel bunks in an industrial barracks. A massive half-giant
    child sitting on one, too big for it. Stenciled number G-12 on coveralls. Machine
    oil smell. Institutional, dehumanizing, the child is just a number.

16. half-giant-scaffold-accident
    Used in: Half-Giant origin, slide 6
    A half-giant catching a falling steel beam mid-collapse on a construction site.
    Their elbow swinging back. A small human figure crumpled nearby. Chaos, dust,
    falling debris. Raw power used to save a life.

17. cyborg-mirror-reflection
    Used in: Cyborg origin, slide 5
    A cyborg pressing a mechanical palm against a bathroom mirror. Half-chrome
    half-flesh reflection staring back. One organic eye visible. Seam lines across
    face where metal meets skin. Identity crisis, loss of humanity.

18. synth-warehouse-rows
    Used in: Synth origin, slide 3
    Rows of identical synths standing motionless in a fluorescent-lit warehouse. Eyes
    open but empty. One being activated by a technician. A single synth at the edge
    looking different — aware, curious, alive. Eerie, mass-produced humanity.

19. synth-rooftop-rain
    Used in: Synth origin, slide 8
    A synth standing alone in the rain on a warehouse rooftop. Water running down their
    synthetic face. City neon reflected in the droplets. Eyes expressing something new —
    wonder, sadness, or the first spark of real emotion. Beautiful, melancholic.

20. shadowkin-blackout-embrace
    Used in: Shadowkin origin, slide 7
    A shadowkin standing perfectly still during a total city blackout. Darkness wrapping
    around them like a living cloak. Everyone else fumbling for flashlights in panic.
    The shadowkin is calm, at home in the dark. Purple-black void energy.

21. shadowkin-shadow-detaches
    Used in: Shadowkin origin, slide 9
    A figure's shadow standing beside them as a separate entity. The shadow is taller,
    with wrong angles, reaching out a hand of pure absence. Eerie void-purple
    highlights. The moment the darkness becomes a companion.

22. voidborn-sensory-overload
    Used in: Voidborn origin, slide 1
    A voidborn with oversized luminous eyes on a crowded megacity street. Translucent
    probability-ghost versions of people branching from every figure like afterimages.
    Sensory overwhelm. Chaotic, beautiful, overwhelming. Purple and cyan energy.

23. voidborn-rooftop-signal
    Used in: Voidborn origin, slide 9
    A voidborn on a rooftop at night, enormous alien eyes wide open to the sky.
    Patterns visible in the star field that no one else can see. A faint cosmic signal
    pulsing down. City sprawl below. Lonely, cosmic, transcendent.
